# Adidas-sales-PowerBI
** Project Objective ** : Adidas Sales Analysis
The objective of this project is to analyze Adidas' sales performance using the provided dataset and uncover key insights related to sales trends, customer behavior, and business performance. The analysis aims to help Adidas optimize its sales strategies and improve decision-making by addressing the following key aspects:

** Problem Statements **:

* 1. Overall Sales Performance
What is the company’s overall performance in terms of total sales, total profit, average selling price, and units sold?

* 2. Profit Contribution by Retailer
Which retailers contribute the highest profit, and which retailers underperform and require strategic attention?

* 3. Sales Trend Analysis
How do sales fluctuate across different time periods (year, quarter, month, and day), and what patterns can help guide planning and forecasting?

4. Product Category Performance
Which product categories drive the highest sales, and how should the company prioritize inventory and marketing efforts?

6. Customer Segment Preferences
7. Which gender and product categories generate the highest units sold, and how can the company better align offerings with customer demand?

8. Sales Channel Effectiveness
Which sales channel—online, outlet, or in-store—performs the best across retailers, and where should the company focus investment to maximize revenue?

9. Regional Sales Performance
Which regions or states show strong sales performance, and which areas require targeted strategies for improvement?

10. City-Level Profitability
Which cities deliver the highest profit, and how can their success be replicated in lower-performing locations?

** Insights of data **: 
The Adidas sales analysis highlights key trends in overall performance, retailer profitability, and customer preferences. It reveals seasonal sales patterns, top-selling product categories, and effective sales channels, helping optimize marketing and inventory strategies. Regional insights further identify growth opportunities for targeted expansion. These findings enable Adidas to make data-driven decisions, improve profitability, and enhance its competitive edge.
